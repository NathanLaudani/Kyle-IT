Building a nice PC isn't for everyone, but it has its perks. You could probably build a better PC using less money than actually buying one. Buying a PC is still a good idea though. If you just want to buy one I reccomend Digital Storm, Cyber Power PC, or Origin PC. If you want accessories for your PC, Razer has some great items for sale. If you don't want a PC or accessories that look as cool or costs as much then Windows has some amazing options for you. and Good? Good. Let's continue.

Before building a PC, make sure you have all the parts. You should get a CPU, Monitor, Motherboard, a Power Supply, Mouse, Keyboard, Memory, Case, Ethernet/ Wifi, or a Hard Drive. You could also get a GPU, a Cooling System, or a Sound Card. Now with all the parts, build it! You can place the parts in the correct places with the game below.
